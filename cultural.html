About Cultural Competition

The Cultural Competition is a vibrant part of the 10th Telangana State Dental Conference, designed to celebrate the artistic talents of our dental community. It serves as a platform for students to showcase their creativity beyond academics, fostering unity, cultural appreciation, and a spirit of healthy competition among future dental professionals.

Multiple Categories

From dance to drama, showcase your talent in various art forms.

Attractive Prizes

Win trophies, certificates, and recognition for your skills.

Dental Students Only

An exclusive stage for UG & PG dental students to shine.

Expert Judges

Performances judged by renowned professionals in the field.

Cultural Categories

Classical Dance

Showcase the grace and tradition of Indian classical dance forms.

Western Dance

Bring the energy with contemporary, hip-hop, and freestyle moves.

Folk Dance

Celebrate our heritage with vibrant and rhythmic folk performances.

Singing (Solo/Group)

Melodious vocals in solo or group categories to captivate the audience.

Instrumental Music

Let your instruments speak the language of music.

Skit / Mime

Expressive storytelling through theatrical skills and silent acting.

Eligibility Criteria

  • Open to UG & PG Dental Students.
  • Valid college ID card is mandatory.
  • Participants must be registered for the conference.
  • Individual & Group participation allowed.

Rules & Guidelines

  • Adhere strictly to time limits per performance.
  • Submit background tracks in MP3 format beforehand.
  • Participants are responsible for costumes & props.
  • The judge’s decision is final and binding.
  • Vulgarity or inappropriate content is strictly prohibited.
