Privacy Policy

1. Information We Collect

We collect various types of information to ensure a smooth registration and conference experience. This includes:

  • Personal Information: Name, Email Address, Phone Number, Professional Designation, and Organization details provided during registration.
  • Payment Information: Transaction details are processed securely through our payment partners. We do not store your credit/debit card numbers or sensitive banking passwords.
  • Technical Data: IP address, browser type, and device information when you visit our website for analytics and security purposes.

2. How We Use Your Information

The information we collect is used strictly for the following purposes:

  • To process your registration and issue your conference entry pass/badge.
  • To communicate important updates, schedule changes, or logistical information regarding the Telangana State Dental Conference.
  • To issue certificates of participation and CME credits where applicable.
  • To improve our website functionality and user experience.

3. Data Protection and Security

We implement robust security measures to protect your personal data from unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. Our database is secured with industry-standard encryption, and access is restricted to authorized personnel only.

4. Third-Party Disclosure

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your Personally Identifiable Information to outside parties. This does not include trusted third parties who assist us in operating our website, conducting our business, or servicing you, so long as those parties agree to keep this information confidential (e.g., payment gateways, email service providers).

5. Cookies

Our website may use cookies to enhance your experience. You can choose to turn off all cookies via your browser settings, but some features of the site may not function properly as a result.
