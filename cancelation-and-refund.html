Cancellation and Refund Policy

1. Cancellation Policy

We understand that circumstances may change. However, due to the logistical planning involved in organizing the conference, cancellations are subject to the following conditions:

  • Cancellations received more than 60 days before the event start date will receive a 75% refund of the registration fee.
  • Cancellations received between 30 and 60 days before the event will receive a 50% refund.
  • Cancellations received less than 30 days before the event are non-refundable.

2. How to Request a Refund

All cancellation requests must be made in writing via email to telanganastatedentalconference@gmail.com. Please include your Registration ID, full name, and phone number in the email.

3. Processing Time

Approved refunds will be processed within 14-21 business days after the conclusion of the conference. Refunds will be issued to the original payment method used during registration.

4. Non-Refundable Items

Certain items are non-refundable, including but not limited to:

  • Accommodation or travel bookings made through third parties.
  • Bank charges or transaction fees incurred during payment.

5. Force Majeure

In the unlikely event that the conference is cancelled or postponed due to acts of God, war, government regulations, disaster, strikes, civil disorder, or any other emergency beyond the control of the organizers, we generally cannot offer refunds. However, we will make every effort to roll over your registration to the rescheduled event date.
