PRESIDENT MESSAGE

10ᵗʰ Telangana State Dental Conference
Hosted by IDA Cyberabad Branch

On behalf of the IDA Telangana State Branch – President & Secretary, IDA Cyberabad Branch – President & Secretary, and the Organizing Chairman & Organizing Secretary, it gives us immense pleasure to extend a warm and heartfelt welcome to the 10ᵗʰ Telangana State Dental Conference, scheduled on 24ᵗʰ & 25ᵗʰ January, 2026.

This prestigious event, hosted by the IDA Cyberabad Branch with the guidance and support of the IDA Telangana State Branch, brings together a vibrant community of dental professionals, academicians, clinicians, students, and industry partners from across the state and beyond.

As the President and Secretary of IDA Telangana State Branch, along with the Organizing Chairman and Organizing Secretary from IDA Cyberabad Branch, we are honored to present a conference that celebrates scientific excellence, professional growth, and collaborative spirit.

Over the course of these two days, delegates will experience a rich blend of scientific sessions, keynote lectures, interactive workshops, panel discussions, and a comprehensive dental expo showcasing the latest technologies, materials, and innovations. With participation from over 3000 delegates and 2000 students, the conference promises to be a remarkable platform for learning, networking, and inspiration.

We sincerely hope this conference not only enhances your knowledge and clinical skills but also strengthens bonds within the dental fraternity. Your presence enriches this gathering and motivates us to strive for higher standards of service, education, and community outreach.

Wishing you an engaging, enlightening, and memorable conference experience.

Warm regards,

About the Conference

Advancing Dentistry Through Knowledge, Innovation & Unity

The 10ᵗʰ Telangana State Dental Conference, hosted by the IDA Cyberabad Branch under the guidance of the IDA Telangana State Branch, is a prestigious annual scientific gathering dedicated to the advancement of dental science, education, and clinical excellence.

This conference serves as a dynamic platform bringing together dental professionals, academicians, clinicians, postgraduate students, and industry leaders to exchange knowledge, explore innovations, and strengthen professional collaboration.

  • High-quality Scientific Sessions & Keynote Lectures
  • Hands-on Workshops & Panel Discussions
  • Participation from 3000+ Delegates & 2000+ Students
  • Comprehensive Dental Expo Showcasing Latest Innovations
