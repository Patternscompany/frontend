About the Conference

Advancing Dentistry Through Knowledge, Innovation & Unity

The 10ᵗʰ Telangana State Dental Conference, hosted by the IDA Cyberabad Branch under the guidance of the IDA Telangana State Branch, is a prestigious annual scientific gathering dedicated to the advancement of dental science, education, and clinical excellence.

This conference serves as a dynamic platform bringing together dental professionals, academicians, clinicians, postgraduate students, and industry leaders to exchange knowledge, explore innovations, and strengthen professional collaboration.

  • High-quality Scientific Sessions & Keynote Lectures
  • Hands-on Workshops & Panel Discussions
  • Participation from 3000+ Delegates & 2000+ Students
  • Comprehensive Dental Expo Showcasing Latest Innovations
